About

I am a post-doctoral research associate in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington, working with Daniela Witten and Jacob Bien.

I received my Ph.D. in statistics in 2018 from Cornell University under the supervision of Jacob Bien. I enjoy thinking about statistical learning problems from both theoretical and computational perspectives. I am now working on developing statistical methods for the analysis of multi-view data.
