About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability at University of California, Santa Barbara. I enjoy thinking about statistical learning problems from both theoretical and computational perspectives. I am broadly interested in high-dimensional statistics and multiple testing problems.

Previously, I was a post-doctoral research associate in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington, working with Daniela Witten at University of Washington, and Jacob Bien at University of Southern California.

I received my Ph.D. in statistics in 2018 from Cornell University under the supervision of Jacob Bien.
